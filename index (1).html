Chivas Soccer Team

About Chivas

Club Deportivo Guadalajara, commonly known as Chivas, is a Mexican professional football club based in Guadalajara, Jalisco. The team is one of the most successful and popular in Mexico, known for its policy of only signing Mexican players.

History

Chivas was founded in 1906 by Edgar Everaert, who was originally from Belgium. The team's colors were inspired by Club Brugge, a team from Everaert's homeland. Chivas is known for only playing with Mexican players, a practice that has set them apart in the world of professional football.

Achievements

Chivas is one of the most successful teams in Mexican football, with a record 12 Liga MX titles, 4 Copa MX titles, and 2 CONCACAF Champions League titles.
